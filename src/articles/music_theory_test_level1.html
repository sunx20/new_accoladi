  • Capo
  • Ballad
  • Aria

  • Two
  • Three
  • Four
  • Six

  • A Modulation

  • Concerto
  • Anthem
  • Etude

  • Flute
  • Tube

  • Cello

8. Scherzo is?

  • Music composed for non-religious purposes
  • A short orchestral composition
  • Another term for a semitone
  • Music composition composed for religious purposes

9. Tenuto and Sostenuto have this in common?

  • Sustained sounds
  • Short and detached notes
  • Gradually slower in tempo
  • Marking for strings instruments indicating that the strings are to be plucked
