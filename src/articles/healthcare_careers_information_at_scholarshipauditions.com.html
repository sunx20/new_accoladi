Healthcare Careers

Are you surprised you can combine a passion for music with your career in healthcare? Here are a few ways to do it with one of the Healthcare Careers listed below.

Audiologist An audiologist is a doctor of audiology (Au.D.) who can diagnose and treat hearing and balance problems.
Bio-Acoustician A bio-acoustician studies how sound is produced, disseminated, and received in animals (including humans).
Music Therapist Music therapists evaluate a variety of human conditions based on the responses a patient gives to music. They are part of an interdisciplinary medical team.
Voice Therapist A voice therapist is another name for a speech-language pathologist. Voice Therapists help people strengthen the muscles and systems used to produce sound.

There are many Healthcare Careers to choose from. This list can be a stepping stone for picking which of the healthcare careers is right for you.
