Audition Advice

Take this simple three question test before we begin the conversation about Audition Advice:

1. Which of the following collegiate Schools of Music have the same audition requirements?

A. Florida State University and New England Conservatory
B. Lander University and Baylor University
C. NYU Steinhardt and Seattle Pacific University
D. None of the above

2. Which of the following collegiate Schools of Music does not require sightreading and/or scales?

A. University of North Texas College of Music
B. Frost School of Music at the University of Miami
C. University of Iowa School of Music
D. They all do

3. Which of the following schools awards scholarship funds at the end of the audition day?

A. University of Michigan School of Music, Theater and Dance
B. Wanda L. Bass School of Music at Oklahoma City University
C. University of South Carolina School of Music
D. None of the above

If you answered D to all three questions, you scored 100. If you did, you probably had to google all the institutions and their audition requirements to find the answer or you made your best educated guess. When it comes to one of the most defining decisions of your life, there should be no guessing.
