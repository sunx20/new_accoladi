Choral Music Education

Standard Audition Requirements

All applicants to collegiate music programs are required to record a standard audition video which includes the content outlined below.

  • Each performance segment must be recorded in one take with no editing (though you may and should select the best of several takes).
  • Professional equipment and/or studio space is not necessary. With attention to a few details, your music making can be conveyed using readily available consumer electronics; even iPhone or other smartphone recordings are acceptable.
  • Please refer to the article “7 Steps in Making a Great Audition Video” for additional information and suggestions for effectively recording your audition.

Required Video Segments for Choral Music Education

Introduction: Before you record your first musical selection, please include a spoken introduction in which you state your name, instrument/voice part, hometown, and state. Then announce the title of the song and composer and/or arranger before you begin the performance for each required tune/style/excerpt.

Musical Selections: You are asked to record only one video. Perform at least two selections for the below requested material:

  • A performance piece of choice with classical implications
  • A musical theater selection
  • A pop or jazz selection
  • An American folksong
  • Perform two of the following songs and accompany yourself on keyboard, (simple chords are acceptable): “The Water is Wide,” “Shenandoah,” “Tale as Old as Time,” “A Whole New World,” and “Let There Be Peace on Earth.”

If you wish to demonstrate proficiency on a doubling or secondary instrument, you must include one or two additional examples.

Required Video Segments for Choral Music Education

Introduction: Before you record your first musical selection, please include a spoken introduction in which you state your name, instrument/voice part, hometown, and state. Then announce the title of the song and composer and/or arranger before you begin the performance for each required tune/style/excerpt.

Required Musical Selections: You are asked to record only one video. Perform at least three selections from the below requested material:

  • A performance piece of choice with classical implications
  • A musical theater selection (preference to a ballad written after 1970)
  • A pop or jazz selection
  • An American folksong
  • Perform two of the following songs and accompany yourself on keyboard, (simple chords are acceptable): “The Water is Wide,” “Shenandoah,” “It’s a Small World,” “Tale as Old as Time,” “A Whole New World,” and “Let There Be Peace on Earth.”
  • (Highly suggested) Using Kodaly/Solfege hand signs and syllables sing:
    • a major scale both ascending and descending
    • a natural minor scale both ascending and descending
    • a harmonic minor scale both ascending and descending
