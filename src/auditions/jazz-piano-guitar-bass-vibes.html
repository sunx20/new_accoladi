Jazz Piano/Guitar/Bass/Vibes Instrumentalists

Standard Audition Requirements

All applicants to collegiate music programs are required to record a standard audition video which includes the content outlined below.

  • Each performance segment must be recorded in one take with no editing (though you may and should select the best of several takes).
  • Professional equipment and/or studio space is not necessary. With attention to a few details, your music making can be conveyed using readily available consumer electronics; even iPhone or other smartphone recordings are acceptable.
  • Please refer to the article “7 Steps in Making a Great Audition Video” for additional information and suggestions for effectively recording your audition.

Required Video Segments for Jazz Piano/Guitar/Bass/Vibes Instrumentalists

Introduction: Before you record your first musical selection, please include a spoken introduction in which you state your name, instrument, hometown, and state. Then announce the title of the song and composer and/or arranger before you begin the performance for each required tune/style/excerpt.

Musical Selections: You are asked to record only one video.

  • One jazz standard of your choice (please provide your own play-along track if desired).
  • The melody of another jazz standard with 1 to 2 improvised choruses.

If you double on another instrument, please include one or two additional examples (jazz standard) of your playing on that instrument. You must submit examples for each doubling instrument listed on your application.

Required Video Segments for Jazz Piano/Guitar/Bass/Vibes

Introduction: Before you record your first musical selection, please include a spoken introduction in which you state your name, instrument, hometown, and state. Then announce the title of the song and composer and/or arranger before you begin the performance for each required tune/style/excerpt.

Required Musical Selections: You are asked to record only one video.

  • Select from the following: “Billie’s Bounce” or “My Little Suede Shoes,” “Now’s the Time,” or “Anthropology” (medium up-tempo): one chorus of melody, two choruses comping or walking, and two choruses soloing
  • One chorus of a ballad—either “Body and Soul” “What a Wonderful World,” “Strange Fruit,” “Mood Indigo,” “Kansas City” or “In a Sentimental Mood”— improvising loosely off the melody
  • Melody and solo from an odd-meter composition—either “Take Five,” “Blue Rondo a la Turk,” “or “Nommo”
  • Guitar Only: Demonstrate comping to a swing tune in the style of Freddie Green.
  • Bass Only: If you play electric bass, please demonstrate one minute of the following:
    • Afro-Cuban or Brazilian
    • Funk or hip-hop

One selection of choice

Strongly Recommended:

Provide an excerpt of your playing in an ensemble—preferably a big band—in either rehearsal or performance. (If not possible due to the cancelation of school due to the Covid-19 Virus then omit this recommendation.)

If you wish to demonstrate proficiency on a doubling or secondary instrument, you must include one or two additional examples.
