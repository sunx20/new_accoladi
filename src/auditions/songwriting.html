Songwriting

Standard Audition Requirements

All applicants to collegiate music programs are required to record a standard audition video which includes the content outlined below.

  • Each performance segment must be recorded in one take with no editing (though you may and should select the best of several takes).
  • Professional equipment and/or studio space is not necessary. With attention to a few details, your music making can be conveyed using readily available consumer electronics; even iPhone or other smartphone recordings are acceptable.
  • Please refer to the article “7 Steps in Making a Great Audition Video” for additional information and suggestions for effectively recording your audition.

Required Video Segments for Songwriting

Introduction: Before you record your first musical selection, please include a spoken introduction in which you state your name, instrument/voice part, hometown, and state.

Musical Selections: You are asked to record only one video. Perform and/or demonstrate at least two selections from the below requested material:

  • A performance piece of choice on primary instrument
  • A song for solo voice with piano or guitar accompaniment
  • A song written in the pop-rock style
  • A ballad written in the musical theater style, pop style, CCM style, and/or Rock or Hip-Hop style
  • A song written in the Rap style
  • A song written in the folk-song style or Country Music style

(A recording of each submitted song is optimal)

If you wish to demonstrate proficiency on a doubling or secondary instrument, you must include one or two additional examples.

  • Professional equipment and/or studio space is not necessary. With attention to a few details, your music making can be conveyed using readily available consumer electronics; even iPhone or other smartphone recordings are acceptable.

Required Video Segments for Songwriting

  • Introduction: Before you record your first musical selection, please include a spoken introduction in which you state your name, instrument/voice part, hometown, and state. Then announce the title of the song and the songwriter before you begin the performance for each song. As songwriter, announce your full name.

Required Musical Selections: You are asked to record only one video. Perform and/or demonstrate at least three selections from the below requested material:

  • A performance piece of choice on primary instrument
  • A song for solo voice with piano or guitar accompaniment
  • A song written in the pop-rock style
  • A ballad written in the musical theater style, pop style, CCM style, and/or Rock or Hip-Hop style
  • A song written in the Rap style
  • A song written in the folk-song style or Country Music style
