Minister of Music/Ministry

Standard Audition Requirements

All applicants to collegiate music programs are required to record a standard audition video which includes the content outlined below.

  • Each performance segment must be recorded in one take with no editing (though you may and should select the best of several takes).
  • Professional equipment and/or studio space is not necessary. With attention to a few details, your music making can be conveyed using readily available consumer electronics; even iPhone or other smartphone recordings are acceptable.
  • Please refer to the article “7 Steps in Making a Great Audition Video” for additional information and suggestions for effectively recording your audition.

Required Video Segments for Minister of Music/Ministry

Introduction: Before you record your first musical selection, please include a spoken introduction in which you state your name, instrument/voice part, hometown, and state. Then announce the title of the song and composer and/or arranger before you begin the performance for each required tune/style/excerpt.

Musical Selections: You are asked to record only one video. Auditionees must be prepared to sing three accompanied songs from the below suggested list:

  • A ministry-style performance of one of the following hymns found in Music for the Master / Beckenhorst Press: “It Is Well With My Soul,” “Amazing Grace,” “Of the Father’s Love Begotten,” “Were You There?,” “Just As I Am,” “Take My Life,” “Something For Thee,” and “O Holy Night”
  • A sacred song of choice
  • An instrumental sacred selection of choice

Optional: Memorization is not required but highly suggested on at least one of the selections

If you wish to demonstrate proficiency on a doubling or secondary instrument, you must include one or two additional examples.

Then announce the title of the song and composer and/or arranger before you begin the performance for each required tune/style/excerpt.

Required Musical Selections: You are asked to record only one video. Auditionees must select and sing three accompanied songs from the suggested list below:

  • A ministry-style performance of one of the following hymns found in Music for the Master / Beckenhorst Press: “It Is Well With My Soul,” “Amazing Grace,” “Of the Father’s Love Begotten,” “Were You There?,” “Just As I Am,” “Take My Life,” “Something For Thee,” and “O Holy Night;” The Deep River Collection /Hal Leonard Publishing: “Deep River,” “Never Said a Mumblin’ Word,” “Give Me Jesus,” “He’s Got the Whole World in His Hands,” “Let Us Break Bread Together,” “My Lord’s Done Been Here,” “Somebody’s Knockin’ at Yo’ Door,” “Sometimes I Feel Like a Motherless Child,” and “Walk Together Children.”
  • A ministry-style performance of one of the following classic religious solo compositions: Malott, “The Lord’s Prayer;” Hine, “How Great Thou Art;” Allitsen, “The Lord Is My Light;” Oxford Solo Songs /Oxford Press: “How Can I Keep from Singing,” “Lord, Make Me An Instrument,” “O God of Mercy,” “Pie Jesu,” “The Lord Bless You and Keep You,” “The Lord’s My Shepherd,” “O Light of Life,” and “Thou Art God.”
  • A sacred song of choice
  • An instrumental sacred selection of choice.

Optional: Memorization is not required but highly suggested on at least one of the selections.

If you wish to wish to demonstrate proficiency on a doubling or secondary instrument, you must include one or two additional examples.
