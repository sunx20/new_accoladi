"My Sample Profile" is a replica of the student profile and data entry forms. You can model your own career and show students step-by-step how to fill in their own profile for maximum impact with college recruiters and faculty.

Getting Started Checklist


  1. Add account information (like your name, school affiliation, contact information, etc.)
  2. Invite students to create accounts
    1. Go to the Students tab on your dashboard
    2. Click “Add”
    3. Fill in their information, and they will receive an email notification
    4. Once the student creates an account, they will be linked to yours
  3. Add students who already have accounts
    1. Ask them to add you from their dashboard
    2. Link the student to your account
  4. Encourage students to add experiences
  5. Encourage students to add videos
  6. Browse your students’ accounts to help them make their profiles the best they can be
  7. Check out additional resources
    1. Grade by grade guide
    2. College search
    3. Non-institutional scholarship search
