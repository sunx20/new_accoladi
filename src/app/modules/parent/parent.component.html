Getting Started Checklist


  1. Add your students to your dashboard. Make sure you subscribe to premium access to get all the benefits of the service
  2. Ensure your student has completed their account information, general performances, private lessons, master classes, and summer camp sections of their profile
    1. Examine your student’s account to determine possible places for improvement, and start taking steps to flesh out their profile
    2. Add as many good quality videos as possible
  3. Make sure your student’s account is fully complete
    1. Speak with your student and help them fill out sections they’re having trouble completing on their own
    2. Add more videos! Record videos in your home showcasing your student’s talent(s)
  4. Check out other resources on Accoladi.com
    1. A guide to the premiere music programs in the country
    2. A summary of careers for people with a music background
    3. A grade-by-grade guide to help your student collect experiences that build their musical portfolio
    4. What parents need to know when their student wants to study music
