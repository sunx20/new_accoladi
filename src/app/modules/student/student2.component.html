Profile Overview

Your profile is the first step in connecting with collegiate recruiters and scholarships trustees. One of the first impressions you will make is through the information shared in this profile. Complete all fields that apply to your musical discipline and keep them updated as you go through middle and high school. You may add to and edit your profile as often as needed.

The required information listed below must be completed for your profile to be visible to higher education recruiters. The optional data is not required, but the more information displayed, the greater the chance to be discovered by prospective colleges, universities, and conservatories.

This information should be reflective of private one-to-one instruction of at least six weeks. Master class participation should not be included in this form. Your class instructor providing after-school help with your ensemble music does not constitute private instruction and should not be included in this section.
