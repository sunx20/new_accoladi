National Signing Day 2021

You have applied to Performers National Signing Day™ You will use this section of Accoladi.com to provide performance requirements. Below, you will see a heading for the "Category" you applied, and an "Add Performance" button.

  1. Add performance information. You are allowed a set number of items based on the category for which you applied.
  2. Make sure to have a URL from YouTube or Vimeo to include. The judging is based on your performance in the video.
  3. Fill in as much data as you can under "My Performances" section of Accoladi.com. Judges will have access to your full profile, so having information filled in can only help you. Plus, you will always be searchable by colleges looking for talent before and after Performers National Signing Day™

Performers National Signing Day™ is an exciting, life-changing, first-of-its-kind nationwide collegiate audition and recruitment event. This multi-day, invitation only event lets high school juniors and seniors showcase their talent for hundreds of collegiate faculty letting schools review performances and offer scholarships directly to students.
