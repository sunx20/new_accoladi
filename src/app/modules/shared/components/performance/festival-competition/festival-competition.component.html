Festivals & Competitions

Solo/Ensemble Festivals and Competitions happen outside of your school, but you might participate in them with your school ensemble. You or your ensemble will get a score, rating, or ranking at festivals and competitions. If you have the judge’s name, your scores, and judge’s comments, add them to each entry.

Here are some examples of what a Solo/Ensemble Festival or Competition might be called:

  • National Association of the Teachers of Singing Solo Competition
  • Steinway National Concerto Competition
  • The South Carolina Band Director’s Association State Solo and Ensemble Festival

This is NOT a Solo/Ensemble Festival or Competition:
Parker High School’s Talent Show where you performed in a trumpet duet and won first-place.
