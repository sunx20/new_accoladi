ABOUT US

Since 2018, Accoladi, the foremost online resource for performing arts students, has been successfully connecting these students with higher education institutions, as well as identifying potential scholarship funds. Prospective music majors or non-music majors desiring to participate in collegiate ensembles are provided with tools, information, and secure exposure to maximize their collegiate potential and scholarship opportunities. Through Accoladi's affordable services, the end result is heightened collegiate acceptance, audition success, and scholarship accessibility.
 

OUR MISSION

To empower student performers to recognize, afford, and achieve their collegiate dreams.
 

OUR STORY

At Accoladi, we believe all students deserve an opportunity to attend a college, university, or conservatory which will strengthen the calling, individual mission, and future career goals of each student. Founder, Dr. Randall Bayne, is an advocate and mentor for expanding performing arts students' access to collegiate opportunities. He has extensive experience working with a myriad of student desires and possibilities as they journey through the collegiate selection, application, audition, scholarship, and acceptance process. Accoladi is a team of professionals who are continually enhancing this service to help students in all musical disciplines find an inviting and affordable path to a higher educational experience.
 

OUR COMMITMENT TO DIVERSITY & INCLUSION

At Accoladi, we are dedicated to providing access and opportunity through the intentional inclusion in our community of students, parents, families, and scholastic and higher education faculty/administrators. We are committed to providing a secure, welcoming, and accessible space for people of any identity or background including, but not limited to race, ethnicity, nationality, gender identity/expression, sexual orientation, socio-economic status, religion, age, and ability. Our service is proactive in learning from, and listening to, all perspectives in the pursuit of our overarching mission.
 

MEET OUR TEAM

ScholarshipAuditions.com is designed and operated by music teachers, former college deans, fine arts parents, and professional musicians.
