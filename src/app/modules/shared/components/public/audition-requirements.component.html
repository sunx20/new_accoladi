Audition Requirements

Knowing what to perform is the first step in preparation for your college audition. The audition requirements give a glimpse of the personality of each school. There are two types of music schools: standard schools offer a generalized collegiate experience, while premier schools have a greater concentration on performance.

Standard School Auditions:

  • Perform a solo of choice
  • Perform two contrasting solos
  • Perform an étude or vocal exercise of choice

Premier School Auditions:

  • Perform solo that is designated
  • Perform solo memorized
  • Perform excerpts

Our in-house research team has reviewed the audition requirements of U.S. colleges, universities, and conservatories and compiled model audition requirements for both standard and premier school auditions.
