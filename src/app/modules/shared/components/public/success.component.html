Success with

Significance

Articles and Assignments

Accoladi has prepared a series entitled “Success with Significance” addressing emotional and relational issues specific to high school musicians. Each article is downloadable and has individual student assignments to bring further clarity to the student’s experience. This could prove to be a motivational boost as students start the new school year. It is suggested that they are presented in the following order:


Finding Your Calling

Research reveals the happiest people are those that have figured out who they are and have dedicated their lives to doing something in which they enjoy and find purpose and mission.
 

 

Motivation Is A Must

Motivation is an inner driving force that pushes people to accomplish things.
 

 

Being a Giver

The meaning of life is to find your gift, the purpose of life is to give that gift away.
 

 

A Competitive Spirit

The greatest winners in life are those who know how to compete against themselves and celebrate the victories they win over who they were yesterday.
 

 

Conquer Your Own Negative Thoughts

Shutting down the negative voices allows your confidence, self-esteem, and energy to flow and to be powerful, not powerless.
 

 

Keeping Your Motivation Fresh

The battle is in the mind before it is ever in the practice room.
