SOAR

Initiative for underserved students

Accoladi recognizes the dreams, desires, and aspirations of performing arts students. The SOAR program was created to allow underserved students the opportunity to take advantage of all Accoladi provides free of charge. This offer is good for the student's complete high school career.

What is

SOAR?

Students from one or more of the following:
  • Title 1 Schools
  • Qualifies for food assistance programs
  • Parent's employment has been impacted by Covid-19
  • Resides in government-subsidized or compromised housing
  • Part of residential group housing or foster care system

Who May Apply

To
APPLY

Simply Click the button below, submit the form on the opened page for consideration. Please fill all fields applicable to your school or organization. Teachers, department heads, or organizational leaders applying, please fill in the “teacher” fields. When accepted to the program, we will communicate the student enrollment process.
Once accepted, encourage your students to complete their student profiles, including performance videos within three months. We will supply supporting documents to assist with this process.
We hope through this collaborative effort, these students in your schools and organizations will find the tools needed to recognize, afford, and achieve their collegiate dreams!
