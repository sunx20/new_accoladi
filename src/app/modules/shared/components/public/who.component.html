WHO WE ARE

Accoladi is a comprehensive online resource for:
  • Middle and high school performing arts students who plan on majoring in music and/or using their abilities to pursue collegiate ensemble participation and possible music scholarship funds
  • Parents, teachers, and administrators who support these students
  • Recruiters from higher education institutions searching for these students

Accoladi propels student connection to collegiate opportunities and provides a vast resource of materials, giving students advantages in the application, audition, and scholarship processes.
