Our Commitment to Teachers

We will connect you to:
  • A platform where your students can upload their educational and performance history, references, and performance videos for collegiate and summer enrichment recruiters
  • A portal to upload and maintain a reference for each of your student’s needs.
  • A platform to follow your students’ collegiate process
  • A college directory of U.S. colleges, universities, and conservatories, and the specific music degrees they offer
  • A directory of non-institutional scholarships to aid your students, parents, and school counselors in the procurement of scholarship funds
  • A directory of music camps, summer stock, and master classes
  • A directory of festivals and competitions
  • A national repertoire directory of solos, appropriate for college auditions
  • Music department entrance exam questions and answers
  • A Resource Center of advice, guides, and musical exercises to facilitate your needs
  • A video series of Motivational Moments that can be used in your classroom
  • A support program (SOAR) for your underserved students
