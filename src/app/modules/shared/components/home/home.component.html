Badges

Badges represent completed profile data that recruiters search against. By making sure you have information in these key areas, you make your profile more likely to be found, and as you continue to gain experience and keep your profile up to date, th emore likely you will be found and contacted.

LOCATION
    Location information is important because Colleges and Universities search based on state and city, and sometimes within a radius from their institution. By having a full address we are able to help searchers find you based on location. Go to your account settings and fill in your contact information.

DATES
    Your graduation year and date of birth are important because Colleges and Universities often search on age or grade. Go to your account settings and fill in your personal information, and go to educational data and fill out your scholastic information.

TALENTS
    Talents are specific needs Colleges and Universities search on. Be as specific as you can be. We give room for ranges when expected. Go to Profile > Talents and fill in your talents information.

SCHOOLS
    Your current school as well as your college prefernces help a recruiter know if you are a fit for their program.

VIDEOS
    Videos are the best way (except for live audition) for you to display your repertoire and abilities to a prospective College or University. In each area of performance, we offer a place to put a link to your video of that performance. Having a good catalog of quality videos will play a part in how a recruiter evaluates your profile. Enter your performances in the correct category in Performances. When you enter data, have a video url to add (YouTube and Vimeo offer free hosting of your video content, and they handle conversion and storage).

HONORS
    Honors and awards performances are gems in your profile! Make sure to list them. Enter your performances in the correct category in Performances.

CAMPS
    Summer Enrichment opportunities are a way to show how motivated you are. Enter your performances in the correct category in Performances.
